POPUP

Poshtel PopUp is a global chain of pop-up luxury hotels designed to provide for its guests affordable suites made of sustainable materials, operated under the Poshtel PopUp Concept.

The hotel can be constructed in a matter of months and removed from a plot without trace. This allows us to provide luxury travel experience to our guests without impacting the environment.

BHUTAN

Bhutan as a mystical kingdom, trouble-free and blissful, nestling in the Himalayas is a place that can remind us of the true meaning of cultural authenticity. Bhutan cut off for centuries to the rest of the world, has now slowly allowed the outside world in with very limited numbers while fiercely guarding its ancient traditions. It remains the only carbon negative country in the world.

Bhutan has also committed to ensuring that at least 68% of its land will be under forest cover in perpetuity. The first project will be on a one-acre plot l with a spectacular view overlooking down into the valley. PALAU

If you don’t like water, stop reading. Palau is an archipelago of about 300 largely pristine limestone and volcanic islands, blanketed in emerald forest, turquoise lagoons, and has become a haven for water sports; from snorkeling and swimming to scuba diving.

Our aim is to create an off-grid sustainable eco-retreat, integrated with the environment, and where visitors can experience living in the jungle, taste locally-grown food, and snorkel by the reef and mangroves within minutes of comfort. With a plethora of hospitality experience and a strong commitment to sustainability, we are fortunate to partner with i&i.

The 50,000 m2 plot with a breathtaking ocean view is located in the emerald jungle, surrounded by untouched flora and fauna, and minutes from beaches, lagoons, and mangroves. Easy access to the capital, commercial Port, and all tourist activities.

FAQ

Here are the answers to some of the most frequently asked questions. Don't see the answer to your question here? Contact us via the contact form below, and we will respond shortly.

What is Poshtel PopUp?

Poshtel PopUp is a hotel concept, designed to provide its guests affordable luxury accommodation, made of sustainable materials. The accommodation units are prefabricated and assembled on spot, to reduce the footprint.

I have a plot. Can I become a local partner?

If you have a land you believe it’s suitable for the hotel, let’s get in touch! If you meet our selection criteria, we will gladly have a dialogue about the potential partnership.

how much does local partner need to invest?

The exact amount depends on the size of the project, but it is expected that the landowner has a financial contribution in the project development.

Who operates poshtel popup hotels?

Most of our landowners have an extensive experience in the hospitality industry, and they will be in charge of the operations. However, if the landowner hasn’t been running a hotel before, such hotel will be operated by third-party operators.
